Frequently Asked Questions

What is Intel Hack?

Intel Hack is an opportunity for you and your team to come together to construct a cool and inventive project in 10 hours!


How big can my team be?

We require all teams to be comprised of 1-4 people. This is different from our normal hackathons to allow for a broader scope of projects.

What if I don't have a team?

No problem! You can either compete by yourself or we will help connect you with other students who are also looking for teammates before and during the event.

Who can attend?

Due to the beginner nature of this hackathon, we are opening it up to all undergraduate students. We will also be allowing non-SCU students to attend as well!

I've never been to a Hackathon!

No worries! We have a team of mentors from Intel to help you along the way. Not only that, this shorter hackathon is targeted towards beginners!

Any other questions?

Feel free to email us at acm@scu.edu if you have any questions or concerns!
